About CleanFit Solutions

CleanFit Solutions is a fictional gym cleaning business created for the SIT774 website project. The site is designed for gym owners, managers, and staff who need dependable cleaning support.

Purpose

The website allows users to browse services, request cleaning support, and send clear enquiries through validated forms.

Target audience

Primary users are fitness centre managers who need professional hygiene services and quick online communication.
