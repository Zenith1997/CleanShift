About our business

A draft website for a gym cleaning business

CleanFit Solutions is a fictional business created for the SIT774 website project. The business focuses on professional cleaning services for fitness centres and wellness spaces.

Purpose

The website helps users understand available services, submit enquiries, review contact information, and move easily between pages using clear navigation.

Target audience

Primary users include gym owners, managers, and staff members who need dependable cleaning support and straightforward online communication.

Business goals

  • Present service information in a clear, professional layout.
  • Make it easy to contact the business.
  • Support future search, feedback, and booking features.
  • Maintain consistent style across all pages.

Design highlights

This page uses readable sectioning, visual cards, spacing, and a consistent colour scheme to communicate the business story in a simple draft format.
